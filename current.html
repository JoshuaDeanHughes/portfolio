Analysis of Montgomery County, MD's Traffic Violations {

Working with Audrey Zinn, Ellie Lewis, and Matthew Farnam we analyzed a traffic violations dataset released by Montgomery County, Maryland. This dataset has over 1.7 million rows and 43 columns of information that we managed using the data visualization software Tableau. The present version of our website includes an introduction, data critique of the dataset, our findings, our conclusion, and several data visualisations.
